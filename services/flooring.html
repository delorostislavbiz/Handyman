Why DIY Flooring Never Looks Right

You watched the video. Tape the edges, roll it on, done in an afternoon. Easy.

Except now you have paint on the ceiling. The corners look streaky. And those brush marks near the trim? They're not going anywhere.

Here's what the home improvement shows skip over. Good flooring isn't just about slapping color on a wall. It's prep work. It's knowing which primer to use on which surface. It's cutting in clean edges without tape bleeding underneath.

Most DIY paint jobs look fine from across the room. But get close and you see it. Drips, holidays (missed spots), visible brush strokes, uneven coverage. The room looks painted. Not professionally painted.

And cabinet flooring? That's a whole different level. Wrong prep means peeling paint within months.

How It Works

Simple Process, Great Results

1

You Pick a Color

Or you don't. We can help with that too. We'll discuss sheen (matte, eggshell, semi-gloss) and what works where.

2

We Prep

Furniture moved or covered. Floors protected. Holes filled. Old paint scraped if needed. Edges taped. This is where the real work happens.

3

We Paint

Primer if needed, then two coats. Cut in the edges, roll the walls. Same crew, start to finish.

4

We Clean Up

Tape removed, touch-ups done, furniture back in place. You come home to a finished room, not a construction zone.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to paint a room?
A standard bedroom takes one day. Larger floors or floors with lots of trim take two days. Cabinets take 3-5 days (multiple coats, drying time between).
Do I need to move my furniture?
No. We move it to the center of the room and cover it. For large pieces like pianos or heavy armoires, we work around them.
What about the paint smell?
Modern low-VOC paints don't smell as strong as the old stuff. Rooms are usually usable by evening. We ventilate as much as possible.
Can you match an existing color?
Yes. We take a chip to the paint store and they match it. Works with any brand.
Do you paint ceilings?
Yes. Ceilings are extra but we recommend doing them if the walls are getting a fresh coat. Makes the whole room look new.
