Handyman Services for Englewood Homes

Englewood has layers. The East Hill section features large luxury homes. Downtown has apartment buildings. And scattered throughout are historic properties, some on the National Register.

26% of Englewood's housing was built before 1940. These aren't cookie-cutter suburban homes. They're Victorians, Colonials, and early 20th century architecture that require careful handling.

What that means for homeowners:

  • Lead paint and asbestos in pre-1960 homes
  • Mix of galvanized, cast iron, and (sometimes) lead pipes
  • Outdated wiring that can't handle modern loads
  • Slate, wood shingle, and clay tile roofs needing specialized care
  • Foundation cracks and settling in 100-year-old structures

We've worked on Englewood homes for years. We know when to preserve and when to update.
